# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Name

God's Life Superstores Database

---

## Background

God's Life Superstores operates multiple retail branches across Nigeria.

The business requires a centralized database to manage products, customers, employees, suppliers, inventory and sales transactions while supporting business reporting and decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives

- Improve inventory management
- Record all customer purchases
- Track employee sales
- Monitor branch performance
- Improve supplier management
- Support business reporting
- Reduce manual record keeping

---

## Functional Requirements

The system shall:

- Store customer information
- Store employee records
- Manage products
- Manage suppliers
- Track inventory
- Record customer orders
- Record order items
- Record payments
- Record returns
- Generate business reports

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- Data integrity
- Fast query performance
- Secure data storage
- High availability
- Easy scalability
- Reliable reporting

---

## Expected Benefits

- Improved operational efficiency
- Better inventory control
- Faster reporting
- Improved customer service
- Better business decision making
